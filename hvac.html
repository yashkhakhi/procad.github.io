HVAC Designing Service

We provide HVAC design services for small and large scale commercial & residential projects. Our goal is to provide efficient Ventilation design by selecting appropriate system on the basis of project requirement. Our experience includes designing Variable Air Volume (VAV) system, Dedicated Outside Air System (DOAS), Energy Recovery Ventilator (ERV), and Chilled beam application.


VENTILATION DESIGN

  • Performing heat load calculation
  • Performing IAQ (Internal Air Quality) and outside air requirement for the building
  • Ventilation system selection and Air Handling Unit selection

DETERMINING DIFFUSERS LOCATIONS AND TYPES

  • Duct Layout using SMACNA standards, duct pressure drop calculations
  • Providing duct accessories as per the codes
  • Equipment schedule

HYDRONIC PIPING DESIGN

  • Calculating required water flow & pipe sizing
  • Heating and Chilled water Pipe layout
  • Direct return or Reverse return piping

HVAC EQUIPMENT SELECTION & LAYOUT

  • Pump and fan selection and specification ascertain
  • Boiler sizing and selection
  • Heating and Cooling Coil sizing and selection
  • Chiller sizing
